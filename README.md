# Serenade

Serenade is a travel website built for married couples and operated by a company dedicated to creating intimate, memorable travel experiences. The site helps couples discover, plan, and book romantic getaways tailored to their interests and relationship stage.

## Mission
To inspire and simplify travel for married couples by curating romantic destinations, experiences, and planning tools that strengthen relationships and create lifelong memories.

## Who this is for
- Married couples seeking romantic weekend getaways, anniversary trips, or longer escapes.
- Couples looking for curated experiences (e.g., private dining, couple’s spa packages, bespoke tours).
- Travel planners, concierge services, and relationship-focused travel advisors working on behalf of couples.

## Key features (concept)
- Curated destination guides with romantic highlights and seasonal recommendations.
- Personalized trip suggestions based on couple preferences (relaxation, adventure, culture, food & wine).
- Experience booking and coordination with vetted local partners and vendors.
- Itineraries designed for two, including suggestions for celebrations (anniversaries, vow renewals).
- Safety, privacy, and accessibility information tailored to couples.

## User experience and brand tone
- Warm, elegant, and reassuring — messaging should focus on connection, ease, and thoughtful detail.
- Photography and visuals should highlight couples, intimate moments, beautiful landscapes, and comfortable luxury rather than crowds.
- Messaging should be inclusive of different ages, backgrounds, and travel styles while centering married couples.

## Design & content guidelines
- Visual identity should use a soft, romantic palette and high-quality imagery.
- Copy should be concise, evocative, and focused on emotional benefits (connection, memorable moments).
- Accessibility: Ensure high contrast, keyboard navigation, readable typography, and alt text for imagery.

## How to contribute / get help

- Report bugs or feature requests to the project maintainers.

For support, feedback, or commercial inquiries, please contact the project owner or company representative.
